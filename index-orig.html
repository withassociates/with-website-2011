Getting here

Our nearest tube is Haggerston on the East London Line; Highbury & Islington on the Victoria Line, and Dalston Kingsland on the Overground aren’t too far either.

Buses that stop within a short walking distance include the 149, 242, 76, 38, 56, 30 and 277. Plan your journey with the Transport for London Journey Planner, entering N1 4EN as the postcode.
